💡 Tips

• Use at least 3 seconds of audio for voice cloning
• Lower temperature = fewer errors but less expression
• More steps = better quality but slower
• Enable "Smooth" if you hear metallic sounds
• Live Streaming plays audio as it generates, no waiting
• Disable streaming to get cross-faded output with Download
• Max Chunk splits long text to prevent OOM
